As of the 2020 season, out of the 767 drivers who started a Grand Prix,[16] there have been 33 F1 World Drivers' Champions. The first F1 World Drivers' Champion was Giuseppe Farina in the 1950 championship and the current title holder is Hamilton in the 2020 season. The title has been won by drivers from the United Kingdom 20 times between 10 drivers, more than any other nation, followed by Brazil, Finland and Germany with three drivers each.
